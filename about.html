About Adviser

Fuad Qurbanov

Born on July 10, 1958, in Baku, he commenced his academic journey in 1984 at Baku State University. Starting off at the Department of Applied Mathematics, he later worked at the Departments of Cybernetics and Information Technologies and Programming. He has gained worldwide recognition in fields such as algorithmic languages, algorithm analysis, and informatics. With 62 articles and 1 patent to his name, this scholar is also the creator of new testing systems and educational concepts. His research spans various domains including computational mathematics, Fuzzy logic, decision-making under uncertainty, and educational methodology. Additionally, he has delved into psychology and the study of thought processes.
